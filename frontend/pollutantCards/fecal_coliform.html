Fecal Coliform

What is it?

Fecal coliforms are bacteria that are associated with human or animal wastes. They usually live in human or animal intestinal tracts.

Where does it come from?

their presence in drinking water is a strong indication of recent sewage or animal waste contamination.

Health effects

Coliform bacteria are usually not harmful organisms, and are only mildly infectious, but if large numbers of coliforms are found in water, there is a high probability that other harmful bacteria may be present.
